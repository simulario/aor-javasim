The Study Game

Links to other games: StudentSIM

This game is based on a simulation of studying a Bachelor or Master program at a university of the student's choice.

Each semester, the student enrolls for courses, attends lectures, submits solutions for assignments and takes exams. The student also has to take care of her every day life. She needs to find the right balance between studying, relaxing and enjoying a social life. Some students may have to secure sufficient income for their living expenses, if their parents do not support them and they neither get a fellowship.

Modeling students, and other persons such as professors, may be based on a general personality model.

There are two possible time models for this game:

  1. In "user time" a semester week is defined by a set of study activities (attending a lecture, submitting an assignment, taking an exam, etc.) with a timeout clock (say, 30 min) for finishing the week. The game can be interrupted any time such that it's state will be saved and restored when the player connects again.
  2. In "calendar time" an in-game semester lasts for 14 real days, such that each day allows one session with a timeout clock (say, 30 min) corresponding to an in-game week, which is lost when the player doesn't connect on that day.

For each semester week the player checks the study schedule and decides what to do.
